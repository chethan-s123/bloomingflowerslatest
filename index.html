Our Values


MISSION: Blooming Flowers Preschool prides itself on the quality of its educational programmes, the professionalism of its staff, the enthusiasm of its students and the high level of support provided by parents and community members. We wholeheartedly serve children and make every moment of learning a joyful, effective and meaningful one. We in Blooming Flowers. Provide everything necessary for the first step of your child, one that takes him or her far in life. We aspire that the children of Blooming Flowers bloom forth as loving persons with good foundation of character and discipline, willingly share their love with others in school, at home and in society. We provide knowledge the child appropriate to their age level in the play way method by catering not only for its physical and emotional development, but by grooming and preparing the child to take the next step of getting enrolled into the reputed educational institutions of the city. Each child is special child for us and we facilitate them to happily grow as they learn with the help of trained staff in friendly environment..
